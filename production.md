# Production Notes

## FX Chaining

A typical FX chain follows:

***GATE/DYNAMICS -> COMPRESSOR -> EQ -> DELAY FX -> REVERB FX***

* **Gate:**
* **Compressor:** Cleans the sound by cutting off loud/high frequencies and low/quiet frequencies set at a certain cutoff level
* **EQ:** Shapes the tone of the sound - can make sound warmer, brighter, thicker, thinner, etc.
* **Delay FX:** things like echo, delay, etc.
* **Reverb FX:** applies spacial reverb
